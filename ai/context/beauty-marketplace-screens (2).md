# 524 Beauty Services Marketplace
## Core Requirements Document

---

## Core Platform Principles

1. Outsource gender safety
2. Makeup max 40 minutes completion
3. Hair service - when receiving hair service, cover/wrap hair and cover body
4. Outsource protection - we also need to protect them, so tell them not to request/give personal information
5. Today's schedule - what is it?

## Screen Flow Notes

Based on the wireframe sketches provided:

### Main Screens Identified:
1. **Welcome Screen** - "524 - Where would you like to get beautiful?"
2. **Service Selection Screen** - Choose Hair, Makeup, or Combined service
3. **Occasion Selection Screen** - Select the event/occasion type
4. **Reservation Screen** - Artist selection and booking
5. **Profile/User Management Screens**
6. **Booking Management**
7. **Artist Portfolio View**
